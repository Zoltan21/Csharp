Welcome to ZDTSS_Transport application.

The purpose of the application is to help the Client (You)  get it's goods 
from a point to another with the help of a courier firm.

The Client actions are :

1.Command:-Add New Command
 	  -View Previous Commands

2.Edit profile

3.View Command status

4.Help (Self explanatory)

1.Add Command will show 2 windows : 
	1st complete the fields with the information required
	2nd window will show when the delivery date will

2.Edit profile gives you the right to change information of your account
